About Tyr Capital

Tyr Capital is a proprietary digital asset trading firm built on the principles of disciplined execution, structured risk management, and deep market research. We do not gamble on volatility; we systematically extract value from it.

Inspired by the Norse god Tyr — the deity of law, justice, and strategic warfare — our firm operates with a precision-first mindset. In an industry often driven by emotion and hype, Tyr Capital relies on data, consistency, and long-term strategic thinking to navigate the complexities of the digital asset landscape.

Strategy

Tyr Capital employs a structured, rules-based approach to digital asset trading — combining multi-timeframe technical analysis, market structure mapping, and macro cycle awareness to identify high-probability opportunities across Bitcoin and select altcoin markets.

Every position is governed by defined risk parameters. Capital preservation is not a secondary concern — it is the foundation. Disciplined entry criteria, pre-determined invalidation levels, and consistent position sizing ensure that no single trade can compromise the broader portfolio.

Research

Tyr Capital publishes independent market research and analysis for traders and investors navigating the digital asset landscape. Coverage spans Bitcoin cycle analysis, macro liquidity conditions, on-chain data interpretation, and emerging developments across blockchain infrastructure — providing the context needed to make informed, conviction-backed decisions.
