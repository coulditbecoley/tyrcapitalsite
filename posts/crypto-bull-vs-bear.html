Bull vs Bear Markets in Crypto

Cryptocurrency markets move in cycles, often categorized as bull markets or bear markets.

What is a Bull Market?

A bull market is characterized by rising prices and positive sentiment.

What is a Bear Market?

A bear market involves declining prices and increased caution among participants.

How to Adapt

  • Adjust expectations
  • Manage risk
  • Stay disciplined

Final Thoughts

Understanding market cycles helps participants make more informed decisions.
