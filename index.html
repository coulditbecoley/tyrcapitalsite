About Tyr Capital

Tyr Capital is a digital asset trading company focused on disciplined strategy development, structured risk management, and market research across cryptocurrency markets.

Inspired by the Norse god Tyr, the company emphasizes discipline, consistency, and long-term thinking when navigating the volatility of digital asset markets.

Strategy

Tyr Capital studies market structure, technical analysis, and macro trends to identify opportunities within cryptocurrency markets including Bitcoin and other emerging digital asset sectors.

Capital preservation, disciplined execution, and risk-defined positioning remain central principles behind every trading decision.

Research

The firm publishes market observations and research covering cryptocurrency cycles, macro developments, price structure, and evolving blockchain technologies.

About Our CEO

CG
Coley Grantham Founder & CEO

Digital Asset Strategist • Manufacturing Entrepreneur • U.S. Army Veteran

Coley Grantham is the Founder and CEO of Tyr Capital, bringing a disciplined, systems-driven approach to digital asset trading forged through over a decade of entrepreneurship and service as a U.S. Army explosive technician. His military background instilled a precision-first mindset that directly informs Tyr Capital's structured risk management philosophy.

Beyond crypto, Coley operates a thermoplastic Kydex holster manufacturing company, applying CNC production, product design, and direct-to-consumer branding at scale. He thinks in systems and leverage — automating workflows, integrating AI tooling, and building businesses structured for long-term growth.
